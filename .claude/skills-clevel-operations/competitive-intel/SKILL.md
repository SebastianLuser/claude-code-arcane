# Competitive Intelligence

Systematic competitor tracking. Not obsession — intelligence that drives real decisions.

## Framework: 5-Layer Intelligence System

### Layer 1: Competitor Identification
**Direct:** Same ICP, same problem. **Indirect:** Same budget, different solution. **Future:** Well-funded startups in adjacent space. Update the 2x2 Threat Matrix quarterly.

### Layer 2: Tracking Dimensions
Track per competitor: product moves, pricing changes, funding, hiring signals, partnerships, customer wins/losses, messaging shifts.

### Layer 3: Analysis Frameworks
SWOT per competitor. Competitive positioning map (2-axis). Feature gap analysis table.

### Layer 4: Output Formats
- **For Sales (CRO):** Battlecards — one page per competitor for pre-call prep
- **For Marketing (CMO):** Positioning update — message shifts, new differentiators
- **For Product (CPO):** Feature gap summary — what to reprioritize
- **For CEO/Board:** Monthly 1-page competitive summary

### Layer 5: Intelligence Cadence
**Monthly:** Review tier-1 competitors, update battlecards. **Triggered:** Funding, launches, pricing changes. **Quarterly:** Full landscape review, positioning map refresh.

## Win/Loss Analysis

Interview every lost deal >$50K ACV, every churn >6 months tenure. NOT conducted by the AE who worked the deal. Aggregate findings monthly: win/loss reasons ranked by frequency.

## The Balance

**Over-tracking signals:** Roadmap driven by competitor launches, morale drops when they fundraise.
**Under-tracking signals:** AEs blindsided on calls, missed major competitor launches.
**Right posture:** Know competitors well enough to win. Don't let them set your agenda.

## Red Flags

| Signal | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| Competitor win rate >50% in your core segment | Fundamental positioning problem |
| Same objection from 5+ deals: "competitor has X" | Real feature gap |
| Competitor hired 10 engineers in your domain | Major product investment incoming |

## Integration with C-Suite Roles

| Intelligence Type | Feeds To | Output Format |
|------------------|----------|---------------|
| Product moves | CPO | Roadmap input, feature gap analysis |
| Pricing changes | CRO, CFO | Pricing response recommendations |
| Customer wins/losses | CRO, CMO | Battlecard updates, positioning shifts |
