# Marketing Context Template

Use this template when generating a `marketing-context.md` document.

## Sections

### 1. Product Overview
- One-line description
- What it does (2-3 sentences)
- Product category and type
- Business model and pricing

### 2. Target Audience
- Target company type (industry, size, stage)
- Target decision-makers (roles, departments)
- Primary use case and JTBD

### 3. Personas
For each stakeholder:
- Role (User, Champion, Decision Maker, Financial Buyer, Technical Influencer)
- What they care about, their challenge, value promised

### 4. Problems & Pain Points
- Core challenge before finding you
- Why current solutions fall short
- Cost (time, money, opportunities)
- Emotional tension

### 5. Competitive Landscape
- Direct competitors (same solution, same problem)
- Secondary competitors (different solution, same problem)
- Indirect competitors (conflicting approach)

### 6. Differentiation
- Key differentiators (capabilities alternatives lack)
- How you solve it differently and why that's better

### 7. Objections & Anti-Personas
- Top 3 objections + responses
- Who is NOT a good fit

### 8. Switching Dynamics (JTBD Four Forces)
- Push (frustrations with current), Pull (attraction to you)
- Habit (what keeps them stuck), Anxiety (worries about switching)

### 9. Customer Language (Verbatim)
- How customers describe the problem/solution
- Words TO use and words to AVOID

### 10. Brand Voice
- Tone, communication style, personality (3-5 adjectives)

### 11. Style Guide
- Grammar, capitalization, formatting, terminology

### 12. Proof Points
- Key metrics, notable customers, testimonial snippets

### 13. Content & SEO Context
- Target keywords by topic cluster
- Internal links map, writing examples

### 14. Goals
- Primary business goal, key conversion action, current metrics
