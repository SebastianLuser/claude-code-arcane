# Marketing Strategy & PMM

Product marketing patterns for positioning, GTM strategy, and competitive intelligence.

## ICP Definition Workflow

1. Analyze existing customers (top 20% by LTV)
2. Identify common firmographics (size, industry, revenue)
3. Map technographics (tools, maturity, integrations)
4. Document psychographics (pain level, motivation, risk tolerance)
5. Define 3-5 buyer personas (economic, technical, user)
6. Validate against sales cycle and churn data
7. **Validation:** A-fit customers have lowest churn and fastest close

For firmographics template, buyer personas table, and ICP validation checklist, see [references/positioning-frameworks.md](references/positioning-frameworks.md).

## Positioning Development

Develop positioning using April Dunford methodology:

1. List competitive alternatives (direct, adjacent, status quo)
2. Isolate unique attributes (features only you have)
3. Map attributes to customer value (why it matters)
4. Define best-fit customers (who cares most)
5. Choose market category (head-to-head, niche, new category)
6. Layer on relevant trends (timing justification)
7. Test with 10+ customer interviews
8. **Validation:** 7+ customers describe value unprompted

### Positioning Statement Template

```
FOR [target customer]
WHO [statement of need]
THE [product] IS A [category]
THAT [key benefit]
UNLIKE [competitive alternative]
OUR PRODUCT [primary differentiation]
```

For value proposition formula, messaging hierarchy, and complete positioning frameworks, see [references/positioning-frameworks.md](references/positioning-frameworks.md).

## Competitive Intelligence

1. Identify tier 1 (direct), tier 2 (adjacent), tier 3 (status quo)
2. Sign up for competitor products (hands-on evaluation)
3. Monitor websites, pricing, messaging; analyze sales call recordings
4. Read G2/Capterra reviews; track competitor job postings (roadmap signals)
5. Update battlecards monthly
6. **Validation:** Sales team uses battlecards in 80%+ competitive deals

For battlecard template, win/loss analysis framework, and competitive tier structure, see [references/messaging-templates.md](references/messaging-templates.md).

## Sales Enablement

Equip sales team with PMM assets:

1. Sales deck (15-20 slides), one-pagers, demo script
2. Email templates, ROI calculator
3. Monthly enablement calls, quarterly training
4. **Validation:** Sales uses assets in 80%+ of opportunities

For sales deck structure, demo flow, and handoff cadence, see [references/launch-checklists.md](references/launch-checklists.md).

## International Expansion

1. Validate market demand (inbound leads, TAM analysis)
2. Localize website, pricing, legal
3. Establish sales coverage, adapt messaging
4. Build local partnerships and references
5. **Validation:** 3+ paying customers from market in first 90 days

For market priority table, localization checklist, and regional playbooks, see [references/international-gtm.md](references/international-gtm.md).
